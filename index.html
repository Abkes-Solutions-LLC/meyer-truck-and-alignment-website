Truck Repair, Roadside Service, Iowa Falls, IA

Meyer Truck & Alignment, Inc has been business for 18 years and Chris Meyer has worked in the industry for the past 23 years. Our goals have always remained the same, to deliver the best possible truck repair for a reasonable price using the best parts and materials possible. Meyer Truck & Alignment, Inc. has been successful because of our honesty and certified mechanics. We service all makes and models of truck, tractors and trailers. Bring in your vehicle today with confidence that it will be serviced or repaired correctly the first time.

Meyer Truck & Alignment, Inc was started by Chris Meyer and his wife Terri on their farm near Iowa Falls, Iowa in 1991 as an Ag equipment repair shop. With a change of vision, the owners realized that the trucking industry seemed more stable and profitable, there by allowing them to be in a position to provide for their family and church.

Meyer Truck & Alignment, Inc. is a family owed full service heavy truck and trailer repair shop. Started in 1991 as a sole proprietorship, we officially incorporated in April of 2005. We believe in supporting our local and regional vendors and suppliers to the greatest extent possible and will continue to do so. We also have a diversified field of suppliers to allow us access to the widest selection of parts and supplies. Our suppliers have proved, over the years, to be both reliable and cost effective. We also sell parts at discounted prices for the do it yourselfers, with the hope that when you do need professional services, you will return and let us take care of your needs.
